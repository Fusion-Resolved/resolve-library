Video categories

Categories shown on the Videos page and in the Add Video form. Edit names, change colours, add new ones or delete any you don't need.

Changes apply immediately. Any videos already saved with deleted categories will still display correctly.

Effect categories

Custom categories that appear on the Effects page alongside the built-in ones. Add your own workflow-specific groupings, change their colours, or remove ones you don't use.

Built-in categories (3D Space, Grade, etc.) cannot be removed here but appear in the effects list automatically. Custom categories added below will also appear in the Edit Effect form.
