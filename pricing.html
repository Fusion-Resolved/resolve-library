Common questions

Anything else? Email support@noding.app

Can I cancel Pro at any time?

Yes — cancel any time from Account Settings. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period. No questions, no dark patterns.

What happens to my effects if I cancel?

Your public effects remain on the platform. Any effects set to Private will become unlisted — still accessible to you, but hidden from public search until you upgrade again or change the visibility setting.

Do you offer refunds?

Full refunds are available within 14 days of initial purchase if you haven't made significant use of Pro-only features. See our Terms & Conditions for full details.

Is VAT included in the price?

UK users will see VAT added at checkout where applicable. EU users will see their local VAT rate. All prices on this page are shown exclusive of VAT.

Is my payment data secure?

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified provider. Noding never sees or stores your full card number — only a secure payment reference and your subscription status.

Do you plan a Team or Studio tier?

Yes — a Studio tier is on the roadmap for post-house and team workflows, including shared pin libraries, team credits, and private organisational databases. Get in touch if this interests you.
